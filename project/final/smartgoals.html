What are SMART Goals?

SMART goals are designed to provide structure and accountability. Each letter stands for a key component of an effective goal:

  • Specific: Clearly define what you want to achieve.
  • Measurable: Include criteria to track progress.
  • Achievable: Ensure your goal is realistic.
  • Relevant: Align the goal with your overall objectives.
  • Time-bound: Set a deadline to stay motivated.
